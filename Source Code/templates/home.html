🔍 What is Named Entity Recognition?

Named Entity Recognition (NER) is a crucial task in Natural Language Processing (NLP) that involves identifying and classifying entities such as:

👤 People

Identifies names of individuals, including celebrities, politicians, and everyday people.

🏢 Organizations

Recognizes names of companies, institutions, and governmental bodies.

📍 Locations

Detects geographical locations such as cities, countries, and landmarks.

📅 Dates

Extracts dates and times, including specific events and historical dates.

🔢 Miscellaneous

Identifies other relevant entities, such as quantities, percentages, and more.

📚 About the Bodo Language

📝 Script

Bodo is written in the Devanagari script, which is also used for several other Indian languages.

🌍 Dialects

The language has several dialects, reflecting the diverse cultural influences in the region.

🏛️ Recognition

Bodo was granted the status of an official language in Assam in 2020, highlighting its importance in the region.

🎶 Cultural Significance

The Bodo language is a vital part of the identity and traditions of the Bodo people, encompassing folklore, music, and literature.
